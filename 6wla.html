6waves Lolapps | Social Game

User Research (cross-cultural comparisons, card sort, user interviews, usability studies), Flash (Adobe), Illustrator (Adobe), Photoshop (Adobe), UX Design (wireframing), UI Design (lo-fi mocks, hi-fi mocks), Prototyping, Iteration

Challenge: Create a delightful user experience and interface that has each game mechanic feature reinforcing each other for a phenomenal user experience on a micro AND macro level.
Solution: Ravenshire Castle. Ravenshire Castle was the third installment of the Ravenworld Games which collectively had over 4 million monthly active users. I led design and worked cross-functionally, and iterated on the UI and UX based on an artful balance of qualitative and quantitative data. I also crafted the first-use/new user experience to create delight and value for our users. As a result, our users repeatedly came back to our game and first-use drop-off was minimized.
