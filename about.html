SAY HI

A San Francisco-based Product Designer focused on both UI & UX. She designs experiences & systems to help humans interact with the digital world.

Born in the gorgeous San Francisco Bay Area, I've lived in such exotic locales as Austin, Texas and Allentown, Pennsylvania. My childhood summers were spent in my Parents’ country where my understanding of different cultures and their interactions with technology blossomed, leading to a keen awareness of differences in behavioral patterns and cultural nuances across the world. Knowing 6 languages and graduating from the University of California, Berkeley, my formal education in design came right after undergrad through the Graphic Design program at the University of California, Berkeley Extension, classes at the Academy of Art in San Francisco, and many years of practical experience. As a Product Designer, I enjoy using metrics to iterate on design, but know the key to a successful product is by artfully balancing quantitative data with qualitative data. I've worked on crafting experiences and systems for both Consumer and Enterprise products. I love working on new features & products, and scaling existing ones. Outside of work, I enjoy giving back by doing small pro-bono design projects for nonprofits. Outside of design, I love video games, French macarons, and tastefully breaking rules.
