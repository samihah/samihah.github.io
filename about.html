I design to empower users, not influence them.

Hello! I'm Samihah, a Product Designer in San Francisco, with many years of experience, focused on designing for value. I graduated from the University of California, Berkeley and have designed for numerous platforms and spaces, from Consumer to Enterprise, Productivity & Analytics to Social Gaming. I've worked on a diverse array of challenges from new user/first-use experience to designing for scale and internationalization. I design delightful experiences and artfully balance quantitative & qualitative data to inform design decisions. I do both UX as well as UI Design, and sometimes I code in HTML/CSS. I have been the first designer, only designer, design founder, and have also worked on design teams. I spent my childhood summers in my Parents’ country where I experienced how different cultures interact with technology - developing a keen awareness of global differences in behavioral patterns and cultural nuances. In my free time, I do design work pro-bono for nonprofits, and speak/mentor/teach others about design. I also love video games, French macarons, hiking, and tastefully breaking rules.
