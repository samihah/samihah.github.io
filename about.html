I design to empower users, not influence them.

Hello! I'm Samihah, a Product Designer with 5+ years of experience, focused on designing for value. I graduated from the University of California, Berkeley and have designed for numerous platforms and spaces, from Consumer to Enterprise, Analytics to Social Gaming. I've worked on a diverse array of challenges from new user/first-use experience to designing for scale and internationalization.I design delightful experiences and artfully balance quantitative & qualitative data. I do both UX as well as UI Design, and sometimes I code in HTML/CSS. I spent my childhood summers in my Parents’ country where I experienced how different cultures interact with technology - developing a keen awareness of global differences in behavioral patterns and cultural nuances. In my free time, I do design work pro-bono for nonprofits, and speak/mentor/teach others about design. I also love video games, French macarons, hiking, and tastefully breaking rules.
