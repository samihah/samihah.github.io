I design to empower users, not influence them.

Hello! I'm Samihah, a Product Designer focused on UX & UI design and designing for value. I spent my childhood summers in my Parents’ country where I experienced first-hand how different cultures interact with technology - developing a keen awareness of international differences in behavioral patterns and cultural nuances. I graduated from the University of California, Berkeley with formal education in design coming after undergrad and through many years of practical work experience. I’ve designed for numerous platforms and spaces, and have worked on a diverse array of challenges from new user/first use experience to designing for scale and internationalization. I design delightful experiences and artfully balance quantitative & qualitative data. Outside of work, I enjoy doing small pro-bono design projects for nonprofits. Outside of design, I love video games, French macarons, and tastefully breaking rules.
