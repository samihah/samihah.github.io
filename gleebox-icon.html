GleeBox | Mobile App Icon

My Tools & Methods: Illustrator (Adobe), Photoshop (Adobe), Visual Design, Iteration

Challenge: Craft a simple, clean, but easily-recognizable iOS app icon for GleeBox that has a subtle level of depth.
Solution: GleeBox iOS app icon. This icon went through many iterations prior to the latest one which is pictured here. I started off sketching ideas before moving to software. I created mocks of various mobile home screens to see what the final versions would look like, and then chose this based on a combination of best aesthetics and easily recognizable within a sea of similar and dissimilar app icons.
