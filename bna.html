Bluenose Analytics | Playbooks

I led design for a startup in the Productivity & Analytics space called Bluenose Analytics and worked on all aspects of the product. Bluenose was a product for customer success managers to track the health of the accounts they're responsible. Based on how our users were using Bluenose, we found the need for the individual contributors to be able to track their work and tie it back to an account. I worked with the Executive team to define the product and worked with Engineering to evaluate our various options after conducting user research. My responsibilities began with the research phase and continued well past delivering high fidelity mocks to Engineering.

Playbooks increased the customer base by 42% and increased stickiness in the product.
