Gleebox | Mobile

User Research (user journey maps, user interviews, usability studies), Product Definition, Sketch (Bohemian Coding), Photoshop, Illustrator, UX Design (wireframing, storyboarding), UI Design (lo-fi & hi-fi mocks), Prototyping, Iteration

Challenge: Easily enabling people to discover unique local goods.
Solution: The Gleebox mobile app. I defined the problem, spec'd out features, crafted the UI & experience, and used a combination of qualitative and quantitative data to understand user behavior to optimize the experience, and iterate on the product. I ran a cross-functional team managing engineering to ensure on-time delivery of product, and content marketing to ensure their content was timely and created loops back to the product for discovery of local goods.
