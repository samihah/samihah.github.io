Gleebox

I started Gleebox out of a need to discover unique jewelry and local goods while traveling. In talking to boutique owners and shoppers that frequent boutiques, I further found that this need extended beyond travel.

In addition to operational tasks, I defined the problem, spec'd out features, crafted the UI & experience, and used a combination of qualitative and quantitative data to understand user behavior to optimize the experience, and iterate on the product. I ran a cross-functional team managing engineering to ensure on-time delivery of product, and content marketing to ensure their content was timely and created loops back to the product for discovery of local goods.
