One Medical | Prenatal Health Care

Every quarter, the Product Dev team hits pause on our core projects and participates in a hackathon. One of the areas in health care that I'm incredibly passionate about is women's health. Women's Health is such a broad topic, that the week before the hackathon, I conducted numerous user research interviews with women about their health care. The range of topics were wide and covered everything from birth control and sexual activity habits to infertility issues, pregnancy, and pregnancy termination. I created two different interview scripts: one for women who have gone through pregnancy recently, and another for women who haven't.

Because many of the topics were deeply sensitive, empathy and building trust were key to running effective user interviews. At the end of the user research session, I narrowed down project options to a prenatal app since we have a prenatal clinical program. I pitched the idea to one of our iOS engineers - she and I then paired the project down in scope to fit it into a 2 day hackathon, which I then designed.
